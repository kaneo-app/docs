Projects are the core of your work in Kaneo. They are the main container for your work, and they can contain multiple tasks, notes, and other resources.

## Creating a Project

To create a new project:

1. Click the "+" button in the sidebar
2. Click "New Project"
3. Enter a name for your project
4. Click "Create Project"

<Steps>

1. Click the "+" button in the sidebar

2. Click "New Project"

3. Enter a name for your project

4. Click "Create Project"

</Steps>

Your new project will be created and you'll be automatically switched to it.
